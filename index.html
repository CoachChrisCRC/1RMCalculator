Method & Disclaimer

The 1RM is an estimated value calculated using the Epley formula: $1RM = W \times (1 + \frac{R}{30})$. These estimates are less accurate at higher rep ranges (over 10-12 reps). Always use caution and proper form when lifting near your maximal effort.
